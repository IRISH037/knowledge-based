Nacpan Beach in El Nido Palawan Island

Nacpan Beach is a four-kilometer strip of cream-colored sand and coconut trees with crystal clear turquoise water. It is located 17th kilometers north of the El Nido town proper in Palawan. This powdery paradise was one of the secret getaways for some tourists until its beauty was discovered by many and eventually became one of the most popular tourist spots in El Nido. In 2016, it was voted the world's 10th best beach by users of travel rating website Trip Advisor.
