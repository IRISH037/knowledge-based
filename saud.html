Saud Beach

This place is perfect as a tourist destination for those of you looking for peace and escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. Visitors can enjoy various exciting activities, like swimming, sunbathing, or just walking around the beach.

The sunset at Saud Beach is a must-see, with the golden-colored sky reflecting off the sea, making for an incredible beach view. The area around Saud Beach also presents other destinations that can be visited for relaxation and exploration, including the Bangui Wind Farm and the Cape Bojeador Lighthouse.
