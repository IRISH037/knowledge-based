Subic Beach

Subic is known for its stunning beaches with crystal-clear waters and white sand. You can spend the day swimming, sunbathing, or just lounging by the water. Some of the best family-friendly beach resorts in the Philippines can be found in Subic.

Subic has a rich cultural and historical background, and you can learn more about it by visiting the local museums and historical sites. It's a great way to add a bit of education to your Subic vacation and discover the intriguing stories of this vibrant coastal town.
