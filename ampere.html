Ampere Beach

Ampere Beach in Dipaculao, Aurora was the first tourist attraction that we visited during our trip to Baler. Unlike your typical beach that's filled with sand, this beach is filled with solid rocks. It's quite a unique sight for a beach and in fact, this is the first and only beach we've seen that has this many rocks. These are no ordinary rocks because theyre all alive. Our guide Leandro told us that he observed how the stones were gradually growing. I guess hes just making us smile because it takes thousands or millions of years before the minerals can solidly form together in a rocks surface to increase its size.
